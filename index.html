Samsung Gear S3

Initial Impressions

    Negatives
  • No enter button

  • Speaker on leftside of watch

  • 4 Initial app downloads on Cyanogen Mod OS

  • Initial setup require Samsung account creation

  • Not very impressive BlueTooth range

  • S/N barely visible on product

  • Rubber watch band

  • Poor battery life ~2.5 days

  • Extemely limited applications versus the Pebble ecosystem

  • Poor water resistance

  • Limited Samsung Fitness "SHealth" activies with no customization

  • Very weak vibration

  • No way to reboot the device?!?!?

  • No built-in stopwatch, 3rd party stopwatches have high battery consumption

  • 3 Days average battery life

    Postitives
  • Robust dial with a lot of potential

  • Amazing screen

  • Wireless charging

  • Spotify integration, but still no download playlist option

  • Bluetooth transmitter, the ability to connect your headphone directly to the device

  • Ability to reply to all sorts of notification built directly into the OS

  • Widget section on homescreen

  • Included 0.7Amp charger, slightly too fast for this battery

  • Standard 22mm watch band
